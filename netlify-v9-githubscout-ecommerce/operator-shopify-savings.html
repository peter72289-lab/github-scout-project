What the scan checks

Most Shopify stores do not have a traffic problem first. They have a stack problem.

Every paid click hits the software choices already sitting on your storefront: review widgets, upsells, popups, bundles, checkout tools, loyalty apps, chat embeds, subscription tools, tracking scripts, and trust badges. Scout turns that stack into a practical decision list.

01 · Cost leaks

Find expensive or overlapping app categories

02 · Conversion surface

Too many embeds and scripts can make a store slower and harder to operate. Scout flags categories to clean up before you install another “conversion” app.
