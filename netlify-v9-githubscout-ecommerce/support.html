Help and onboarding

Support

For billing, onboarding, storefront scan questions, refund requests, or agency pricing, contact the GitHub Scout team with the checkout email and storefront URL involved.

Launch Support Inbox

Email: support@githubscout.ai

Use this address for refund requests, scan follow-up, billing questions, account changes, and agency pricing.

What to Include

  • Your checkout email.
  • Your Shopify or ecommerce storefront URL.
  • Your plan: Operator, Director, or Agency.
  • The issue or decision you need help with.

Expected Response

During launch, support is handled manually. Priority goes to billing issues, failed checkouts, paid subscriber onboarding, and refund/cancellation requests.
