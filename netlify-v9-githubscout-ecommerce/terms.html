Terms of Service

GitHub Scout provides ecommerce software discovery, public storefront analysis, and recommendations for operators deciding which plugins, embeds, widgets, and third-party tools to use.

Plans and Usage

  • Operator is $17/month and includes analysis for up to 10 storefronts per month across all 15 sources.
  • Director is $37/month and includes analysis for up to 30 storefronts per month across all 15 sources.
  • Agency or higher-volume usage requires custom pricing through the agency contact flow.

Billing

Subscriptions are processed by Stripe and renew monthly until canceled. Customers are responsible for keeping payment details current and for canceling before the next renewal date if they no longer want access.

Acceptable Use

Submit only public storefront URLs you are authorized to evaluate. Do not use GitHub Scout to scan private systems, local networks, internal tools, or URLs that are not public ecommerce storefronts.

No Guaranteed Outcome

GitHub Scout provides recommendations and decision support, not guaranteed savings, conversion lift, legal advice, financial advice, or security certification. Operators remain responsible for testing changes before implementation.

Cancellation

You may cancel future renewals through Stripe customer support flows or by contacting support. See Refunds and Cancellation for the launch guarantee.
