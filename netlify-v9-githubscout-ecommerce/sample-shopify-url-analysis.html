10-day software action plan

Day 1-2 · Keep / remove
Inventory every app and script touching product, cart, checkout, email capture, review proof, and post-purchase flow.

Remove inactive widgets and old promo embeds first. This is the cleanest savings win.

Day 3-4 · Swap
Consolidate review proof into one widget with photo reviews, Q&A, and product-level placement.

This reduces overlap and makes the proof surface easier to optimize.

Day 5-7 · Test
Add one bundle or quantity-break test on the top-selling product template.

Prioritize AOV lift before buying another broad CRO app.

Day 8-10 · Measure
Track app spend, speed, add-to-cart rate, and checkout progression before the next subscription cycle.

The goal is lower software cost plus a cleaner conversion surface.
