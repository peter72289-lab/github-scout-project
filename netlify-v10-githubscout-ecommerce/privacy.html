Privacy Policy

GitHub Scout collects the minimum information needed to run the ecommerce URL analyzer, process subscriptions, and follow up with operators who request a scan or agency pricing.

Information We Collect

  • Email address, storefront URL, monthly spend range, submitted notes, UTM parameters, landing page, and referrer.
  • Public HTML and public software signals from submitted storefront URLs for the purpose of generating recommendations.
  • Checkout and subscription metadata handled by Stripe. GitHub Scout does not store full card numbers.
  • Basic analytics events such as CTA clicks, form submissions, and thank-you page views.

How We Use It

We use submitted information to generate storefront analysis, route leads and customers into follow-up workflows, improve the landing page and scanner, support customers, and maintain subscription records.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers needed to operate the business, including Stripe for payments, Netlify for hosting and functions, and CRM or workflow tools used for onboarding and support. We do not sell personal information.

Retention

Lead and customer records are kept as long as needed for support, billing, legal, and product improvement purposes. Storefront scan data may be retained to improve recommendations unless deletion is requested.

Your Choices

You can request correction, export, or deletion by contacting support through the Support page. Marketing emails include an unsubscribe option when email automation is active.

This plain-English policy is a launch readiness document, not a substitute for attorney-reviewed privacy terms for your final production domain.
