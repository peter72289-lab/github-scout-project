Refunds and Cancellation

GitHub Scout is sold as a monthly subscription through Stripe. The launch offer includes a simple first-month guarantee so operators can evaluate the product without a long commitment.

Money-Back Guarantee

Operator and Director subscribers can request a refund of the first paid month within 14 days of the first charge if they are not satisfied with the analysis or recommendations.

How to Request a Refund

Contact support with the email used at checkout, the plan name, and the reason for the request. Approved refunds are processed back through Stripe.

Cancellation

Cancellation stops future renewal charges. It does not automatically refund past periods outside the launch guarantee unless required by law or approved by support.

Agency Plans

Agency and custom plans may have separate terms based on volume, workflow, and service commitments.
